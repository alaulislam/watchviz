What the visualizations shows?
Here the bar charts display weekly sleep duration in hours on smartwatch screen.

Why we should care about it?
While the performance and display quality of smartwatches keep improving, the size of the wearable imposes notable limits on the display and interaction possibilities with more complex content. In this prototype I propose a selection technique where user can transform one visualization to another form for precise selection input.

HOW?
The instructions of the selection process is described in the sticky notes above.

Improvements:
Current data is simplified and constructed for easy implementation. Sleep data for 7 days is being visualized for testing purpose. Same problem will be faced if we want to interact with more than 7 days data e.g monthly data. So, the Improvement can be sliding/hovering or even displaying the feedback in dynamic relative positions.
